Wesley Harper

I had a lot of fun doing this project. The client did not give much information about how he wants the site to look like, so I just go off my creativity.

His logo is very good, it's simple and has a lot of characters in it. Therefore, I just went off the idea from his logo, made the site with sharp edges and wide cta boxes. He also wanted to have an SVG map, so I just sketched the SVG map, and added in hover effect with jQuery, to have the area lighted up when user hover to the shape or the area's name.

It was a fairly easy project. The client was very happy with the site, and he went live with it very soon after I'm done with the site.

Lessons that I learnt:

  • Responsive design for cta boxes
  • Improve a very simple design in an elegant way
  • Making SVG interactive map
